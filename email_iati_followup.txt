2. ACTIVITY STATUS COUNT — resolved ✅

Once we apply the same non-multinational filter, our counts match d-portal exactly:

SELECT act.status_code, COUNT(DISTINCT act.aid)
FROM act
JOIN country ON country.aid = act.aid
WHERE country.country_code = 'KM'
  AND (SELECT COUNT(*) FROM country c2 WHERE c2.aid = act.aid) = 1
GROUP BY act.status_code

Results: Implementation 332, Closed 355, Finalisation 246, Pipeline 76, Cancelled 1 — identical to d-portal. ✅

3. SECTOR 12110 COUNT — resolved ✅

SELECT COUNT(DISTINCT act.aid)
FROM act
JOIN country ON country.aid = act.aid
JOIN sector ON sector.aid = act.aid
WHERE country.country_code = 'KM'
  AND (SELECT COUNT(*) FROM country c2 WHERE c2.aid = act.aid) = 1
  AND sector.sector_code = '12110'

This gives 112 distinct activities. The figure of 119 shown on d-portal appears to count sector entries rather than distinct activities. We assume this is expected behaviour — please confirm if possible.
